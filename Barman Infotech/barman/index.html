OutSourcing

Outsourcing is "an agreement in which one company contracts-out a part of their existing internal activity to another company". It involves the contracting out of a business process (e.g. payroll processing, claims processing) and operational, and/or non-core functions (e.g. manufacturing, facility management, call center support) to another party (see also business process outsourcing).

Why Outsource with us??

  • Barman Infotech Pvt. Ltd. understands your requirement and employs an outsourcing strategy to focus on core aspects of your business.
  • We strive to improve efficiency, streamlining and productivity of your organization while you can take care of other necessary tasks.
  • It will lead to faster turnaround times, increased competitiveness within an industry and the cutting of overall operational costs.
  • Optimized labour cosrs, while simultaneously growing to have access to technology without investing large amounts of money to own the technology.

Insurance

Insurance is a means of protection from financial loss. It is a form of risk management primarily used to hedge against the risk of a contingent, uncertain loss. Insurance policies, a contract between the policyholder and the insurance company, are of different types depending on the risk they mitigate. Broad categories include life, health, motor, travel, home, rural, commercial and business insurance.

Why choose Barman Infotech Pvt. Ltd. ??

  • We study various insurance policies of our partners extensively so as to provide best solutions.
  • we provide full support for any cancellations and endorsements.
  • We compare various plans so that we can provide extensive support to our customers.
